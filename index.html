Profesional Profile

In 2012 I started to the computer engineering department of Sakarya University. I have been trained in hardware, network, and software, especially for .net technologies for 4 years. In 2016, I graduated with a platformer game for iOS.

Then I worked as an iOS developer for 14 months at Apphic Limited. My job was planning the development process, designing UI/UX, developing mobile applications and updating the applications we developed.

I moved to YGA research and development department shortly after I worked on navigation SDK we developed for visually disabled people in Poi Labs.

I'm currently leading the iOS Applications of My Dream Companion, WeWALK and twin in YGA.
